Greyson

America Cerebral Palsy

I wouldn't change Greyson for the world, but I want to change the world for Greyson.

Greyson is an in utero cerebellum pediatric stroke survivor who has Cerebral Palsy. His challenges revolves around his balance and coordination. He was non verbal and walking with assistance for the longest time. In January of 2018, he became fully verbal and walked independently!

CP doesn't stop or slow Greyson down. He is a fun, loving, silly, yet determined little boy. He has the sweetest and kindest heart. I truly believe and show him everyday that he can do anything that he puts his mind to.

In the words of Drew Brees 'You can accomplish anything in life, if you work for it.' We truly live by that statement.
