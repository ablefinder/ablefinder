Sophie

United Kingdom Down syndrome

Sophie is a charismatic, friendly little girl that has a really zest for life!

She has a beautiful, cheeky personality and everyone that meets her loves her.

She is very confident and knows what she wants a really determined character.

She adores sports and performing arts. She is doing extremely well at mainstream school and also models.

Down syndrome is part of Sophie, but doesn't define her, Sophie sure rocks her extra chromosome. We greatly believe in focusing on the ability not the disability.
