Grace

Australia Autism

This is our Grace, our Amazing Grace.

She is 5 years old and has Autism, Sensory Processing Disorder, and Developmental Delay. She might be non-verbal, but she has an infectious smile and lights up every room she walks into.

Grace is strong willed, determined, and tries so hard to be independent. She amazes us all the time with her learning and we adore her for her for her kind and strong heart, and copious amounts of loving energy.

Grace loves Bananas In Pyjamas, music and dancing, playing outside on the swing and on and with anything that takes her fancy.
