Daniel

United Kingdom Williams Syndrome

What makes Daniel much more than his Williams Syndrome diagnosis?

Love.

Daniel may be missing a part of a chromosome but he has more than enough love to make up for it.

He is much more than his diagnosis. He is able to do anything he desires. The world is his.

He faces such tough medical issues like two heart defects, hearing loss and undescended testes. However, he will not be broken. He smiles at the world even on the toughest days.

He is a life changer and the world is a much better place because he is in it.
