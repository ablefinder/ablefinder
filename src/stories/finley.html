Finley

Australia Cerebral Palsy

Finley has the ability to light up a room with her smile. She is courageous and caring and so full of love. Our little warrior has proved every doctor wrong to date and continues to prove to the world her diagnosis is a term on paper and not what defines her.

Finley is a dancer, a singer, a painter, a little girl full of love and laughter, and she has quadriplegic cerebral palsy and an acquired brain injury. Those things are a part of her but not the whole story.
