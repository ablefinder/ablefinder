Wyatt

America Christianson Syndrome

Wyatt was diagnosed with Christianson Syndrome a month before his 2nd birthday. We thought he’d never talk, run, jump, and many other things. He is almost 4 and he can say 40 words, run, jump, climb, and so much more! He surprises us daily and I’ve learned to never expect anything less from him!
