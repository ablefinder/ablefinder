Crystal

America Autism

I was born with Autism spectrum disorder savantism and sensory processing disorder. I am an international ambassador, self advocate, global messenger for Special Olympics, the founder of a non profit organization called Changing the stigma of disabilities, a public speaker, and the National Miss Amazing Queen.

I never let my disability stop me from reaching my goals and dreams. I intend to be the voice for those who can't use their voices. I love building other people up and proving to the world that I am strong and can do anything I set my mind to.

I am what Autism looks like and I'm very proud of who I am.
