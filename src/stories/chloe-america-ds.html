Who is Chloe Jaye?

She is beautiful, thoughtful, hilarious, empathetic, considerate and full of joy. Chloe has eyes that shine and a smile that can brighten your day! Chloe works hard in so many areas of her life and teaches others to not give up!

She loves people well, yes even at a young age, and blesses everyone with her presence! Hugs and kisses are her number one priority and we are so fortunate to be the daily recipients of these!
