Arianna

Australia Sotos Syndrome

Ari was born with low blood sugars, low tone and has overcome a feeding tube, emergency heart surgery, sleep apnea, conductive hearing loss and gross motor and speech delays. She wears a brace 20 hours a day due to scoliosis and her early bone growth makes her the size of a 4 year old at 2.5 years old.

Ari is an affectionate, happy girl who loves her family. She's overcome a lot and come so far. With the right support, she has a bright future ahead!
