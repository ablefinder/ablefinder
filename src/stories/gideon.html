Gideon

Phillipines Cerebral Palsy

Our son with cerebral palsy is the third of four boys. While we celebrate his brothers’ accomplishments, our family equally rejoices in this special young man’s developmental milestones no matter how few and far between they come.

Through Gideon James, we have learned to always look at the bright side of life and not allow challenges to get us down. He has taught us a lot in terms of patience, contentment, happiness, hope, and faith. You may read more about his story in the book, “Embracing Disability This Ability.”
