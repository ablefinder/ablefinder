Oliver

United Kingdom Leukodystrphy

This is Oliver. The happiest, most determined and sweet natured little boy. You wouldn’t know it, but Oliver is also epileptic, disabled, non-verbal and has an undiagnosed life limiting condition.

Oliver brings so much joy to so many people and the progress he makes in light of his difficulties leaves me in awe. My heart bursts with pride on a daily basis. Oliver is not only my son but my best friend, my hero and above all, he is able.
