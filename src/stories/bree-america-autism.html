Bree

America Autism

When I was 5 years old, I was diagnosed with Autism. That label helped me to understand my challenges, but it didn’t stop me from being the person I was born to be!

I am a college student, an employee, a public speaker, a singer, a Special Olympics athlete, an advocate and a college cross country and track athlete, just to name a few.

I want the world to see all those with challenges can do. I want to make a difference in this world, while staying true to ME!
