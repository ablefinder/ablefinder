Dahlia

America Williams Syndrome

This Dahlia. She is a sweet, fun, funny, smart, seven year old girl with Williams Syndrome.

Dahlia has never met a stranger, adores the ocean, has an extremely strong connection to music, and loves to dance! She has a younger brother and sister who she adores.

Dahlia has had the amazing experience of being a part of a beautiful full inclusion classroom and it has been such a wonderful experience for all! She adores her friends and they adore her.
