Aden

Canada Williams syndrome

Being your parent is the most challenging and rewarding calling I've been given. You are not your challenges. You are your abilities. Your willingness to be brave and overcome your anxieties and fears with encouragement is noble and uplifting.

I catch myself doubting your abilities but you tend to prove me wrong each and every time. Your ability to see the world with non-bias eyes and befriend those in your space is an incredible gift. Your extreme emotions can stop me in my tracks and your caring vocabulary is out of this world.

You are not your challenge of having Williams Syndrome. You are You. You are able.
