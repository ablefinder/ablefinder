Chloe

Scotland Down syndrome

Chloe is 5 years old, has gorgeous long hair, blue eyes and has lost 2 baby teeth so far. She LOVES to play with her dolls, and dressing up like a Disney princess! Her favourite colour is blue, she has Down Syndrome, and loves sausages and pizza! Chloe also goes to dance classes (ballet, Pom & lyrical) and she is also a signed model and actress. She sings literally all of the time, and enjoys going to school.

See the words Down syndrome? See how small they look in that description of Chloe? See how those words are just a small part of Chloe, and NOT a definition of who she is?
