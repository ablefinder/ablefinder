amanda

Sweden Hydrocephalus

I call her my “lucky charm”.

My little princess was born with hydrocephalus. The doctors told me to abort her when I was 7 months pregnant because they thought she wouldn’t survive for more than a couple of months and would make my life miserable. That was 4 years ago…

Amanda is the strongest girl I have ever come across and has taught me to be strong. Seeing her life change from pain to smiles has given me life.
