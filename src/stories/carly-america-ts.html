Carly

America Turner syndrome

My name is Carly and I was diagnosed with Turner Syndrome when I was four years old. I may be missing a chromosome but I am not missing out on life!

I love school, softball, drama club, and hanging out with friends.

My disorder has only made me stronger. I have surpassed many milestones that doctors said weren't possible. Each day is a new opportunity to keep proving the doctors wrong. I am determined, brave, loving, and able.
