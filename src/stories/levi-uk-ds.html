Levi

United Kingdom Down syndrome

Where to start with our Little Levi! I could gush about this boy all day but I will try and keep it brief.

Watching Levi blossom into a snotty, funny, stroppy, stubborn cuddlebum has been nothing but a pleasure! If I'm honest...it's been no different really to raising Levi's older brother. Levis milestones come a bit later but it also teaches you not to take things for granted!

Levis personality and beautiful smile shines far beyond his diagnosis BUT be warned he will lure you in with his big blue eyes then squeeze your face with his super strong grip!!!
