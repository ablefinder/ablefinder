Renée

Australia Severe rheumatoid arthritis

No limits.

A Erin Clark once said, sometimes people see my wheelchair and invent limitations for me. Then, when I overcome those limits just by being me, they are surprised or 'inspired'. I'm just doing my life. I work, play, socialise, go to the movies, dinner, drinks, enjoy sport, learn, teach, give, and many other things just like you.

My wheelchair isn't my limitation, it's my enabler. What limits me is people's attitude and the way we create the physical world. Due to this I will always contribute to discussions and awareness raising because we can all do something to turn disability in to ability.
