Eli

America Traumatic brain injury

Eli has the ability to light up any room he comes into and can make even the grouchiest person smile. You never see him without a smile that uses his whole face...his bright blue eyes, his silly eyebrows and of course that mouth that’s wide open with his signature smile.

He loves to “sing” and “talk” to people in his own way, you just have to listen with your heart instead of your ears and you’ll know what he’s saying. His absolute joy in life is probably one of my very favorite things about him.
