Roman

Austria Epilepsy

This is Roman. He's my adventurous, curious, spunky, and lovely little boy. He was diagnosed with epilepsy 3 weeks before his 3rd birthday, and although he has gone through so much this past year, he still wakes up each morning with a huge smile on his face.

Roman is radiant and full of life and he lights up the room with his laughter. He amazes me each and every day with his courage and determination, and I know he is going to continue to move mountains.
