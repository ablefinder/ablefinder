Liam

America Down syndrome

This is Liam! His smile is infectious! His personality as big as the earth! He doesn’t meet a stranger and is full of hugs and handshakes. Liam is polite and friendly and able to do anything he sets his mind to.

He loves to swim, participate in his run club and choir, and do anything any other 6 year old boy does. He is currently in 1st grade and is impressing his father and me everyday with all he is learning.

My favorite part of the day with Liam is the morning. Every morning he greets me with a giant hug and a “Hi Mom” in the sweetest voice I’ve heard. I’m honored to be his mom!
