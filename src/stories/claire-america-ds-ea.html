Claire

America Down syndrome, Esophageal atresia

Claire was born with Down syndrome, multiple heart defects, and a rare disorder called esophageal atresia. Despite having 20 surgeries by age 2, she is absolutely thriving!

Claire is extremely social, adores her big sister, loves music, and is almost walking. She has taught us so much about perseverance and seeing the beauty in the world that others may not be able to see. We are so thankful God gave her to our family. We also hope that others who may face a similar prenatal diagnosis will not let fear prevent them from experiencing one of the biggest blessing of their lives.
