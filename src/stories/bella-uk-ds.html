Bella

United Kingdom Down syndrome

Bella loves food, her sensory toys and trying new things!

Bella is very independent and likes to do everything by herself. She is also very determined, which I think are brilliant traits for her to grow up with.

Down syndrome doesn’t define Bella. It isn’t who she is, but just a part of who she is. Down syndrome won’t stop her from loving and living life to the fullest!
