Jaxon

United Kingdom Autism

Jaxon is a very chatty 7 year old! He can ride a two wheeled bike, and he can swim - things professionals said he wouldn’t do! He came top in his class in maths! Jaxon brightens our days and we are proud of every little milestone he achieves!

Autism is a small part of Jaxon but he never lets that stand in his way!
