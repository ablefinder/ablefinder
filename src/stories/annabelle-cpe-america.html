Annabelle

America Cerebral Palsy & Epilepsy

Annebelle is the happiest kid I've ever met, despite being diagnosed with cerebral palsy, epilepsy, incontinentia pigmenti, developmental delay and apraxia. She works her little buns off in therapy multiple times a week almost always without complaint. I have never heard my daughter tell me she loves me or talk about her day but she constantly finds ways to show me.

Annebelle spends her days rocking her babies, singing in her Frozen microphone and watching Moana. She plays with her big brother and loves every minute of racing around the house and dragging out 100 toys.
