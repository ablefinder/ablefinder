Ella Stephens

Australia Cri du chat Syndrome

When Ella was diagnosed with Cri du chat Syndrome she was only 3 weeks old. I was told to expect the worst, no crawling, no walking no eye contact, no communication!

I never gave up on her and with a lot of hard work Ella have achieved all that and more! Ella's disability does not define who she is.

Ella is cheeky, social, happy, rambunctious and amazing! There's no stopping her!
