Quinn

America Spondyloepiphyseal dysplasia

Quinn is a positive force who loves to meet new people, dance, sing, and hang out with her friends. Prior to her birth we were told that Quinn would not survive more than a few minutes. Fourteen years, and as many surgeries later, she continues to defy expectations!

Because of her orthopedic challenges and the fact that she is just 86 cm tall, Quinn uses an electric scooter to get around. Her diagnosis of spondyloepiphyseal dysplasia (a form of dwarfism) has led us to raise awareness about dwarfism and to connect with wonderful people all over the world!
