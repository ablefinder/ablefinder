Do something great for your business and for families living with disability. Inspire your staff, clients, and customers through a tailored partnership with us.

Pro bono support

Many organisations choose to support us in ways other than financial, such as through staff fundraising and commercial partnerships.

Donating your products, services, or time is an easy way to provide us with valuable assistance.

By offering your professional skills, such as mentoring, training, or other similar intellectual assets, you can help to achieve your corporate responsibility goals as well as demonstrating your commitment to our cause.

This pro-bono support allows us to use our fundraising dollars even more effectively.
