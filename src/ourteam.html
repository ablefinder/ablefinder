Meet the leadership team

Summer Elizabeth

CEO & Co-founder

Summer Elizabeth is a qualified Speech Pathologist passionate about community and driving positive social change.

Summer brings with her 10 years of industry experience, a deep understanding of the problem of social isolation, and a strong personal connection to the disability community.

Prior to founding AbleFinder, Summer worked at a school for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder, as part of their transdisciplinary therapy team.

Andrius Petrošius

CTO & Co-founder

Andrius Petrosius is a serial entrepreneur on a mission to see the power of technology serve the well-being of humankind.

Andrius’ entrepreneurial journey started at the age of fourteen when he built a Dutch social network that grew to 50,000 users in the first two years of operation. Fifteen years later, Andrius has an established agency and brings with him a diverse portfolio of tech product design and development.

Andrius describes himself as curious and driven, with a long history of strong leadership, strategic partnership, and out-of-the-box thinking.
