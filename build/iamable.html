Because disability does not mean inability.

The #IAMABLE campaign is a digital portrait gallery featuring the faces and stories of real people living extraordinary lives. Coming from all over the world and with all sorts of experiences, the group of changemakers featured in the gallery are proving that they are capable of so much more than the can’t or won’t-dos that are much too quickly attached to their diagnostic “label”.

Run in December, during International Day of People with Disability, the #IAMABLE campaign is helping workplaces, schools, and communities to smash stereotypes and create a new dialogue around disability.
